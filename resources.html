    Study Habits

  • Watch at least one coding video every day, plus as many extra as I feel like
  • Google or search YouTube for answers to my questions (hint: include the keywords "html css")
  • Try to make most or all the study groups, and if I don't have any questions, just use that time to practice or learn on my own.
  • Try to share anything I learn with my fellow students, because teaching what I've just learned helps my own learning process.
  • Experiment, experiment, experiment!
